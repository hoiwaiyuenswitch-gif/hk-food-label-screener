Hong Kong & Macau Food Label Screener

A second pair of eyes for prepackaged food labels: paste the label text as-is and get review flags, each with its trigger, regulatory anchor, anchor tier, and judgment status. The tool raises flags; a qualified human decides.

Version 2.1 (Phase 0 refactor + fix round) · Built 2026-07-08 · Not formally validated
Read this first. This tool only raises flags for review — it never judges, grades, approves, or rejects a label. It sees only the text you paste: it cannot see the physical package, and it cannot check ingredient amounts, what is inside compound ingredients, or whether a claim is substantiated. Every finding — and anything the tool did not flag — must be reviewed by a qualified food-safety professional. It is a screening aid, not legal advice, and it has not undergone formal validation.
